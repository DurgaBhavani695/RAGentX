# The History of RAGentX Corp

## 🌟 Vision
Founded in the spring of 2026, RAGentX Corp was established with a singular mission: to bridge the gap between static data and active intelligence through autonomous agentic workflows.

## 📈 Milestones

### Q1 2026: The Genesis
A team of AI engineers identified a major flaw in traditional RAG: "One-shot" retrieval often leads to irrelevant context. They began developing the **Self-Correction Retrieval Loop**.

### Q2 2026: Launch of RAGentX v1.0
The first production-ready version was released, featuring the **LangGraph Orchestrator** and **Hybrid Ensemble Retrieval**. This version achieved a 40% increase in retrieval precision compared to standard vector search.

### Q3 2026: Enterprise Expansion
Major financial institutions adopted RAGentX for its **Transparent Traceability**, allowing compliance officers to audit the exact reasoning path of every AI response.

## 🏢 Global Presence
RAGentX Corp is headquartered in Neo-San Francisco, with regional "Agentic Hubs" in London and Tokyo.

## 🔬 R&D Focus
The company's current research focuses on **Multi-Modal Agentic Reasoning** and **Zero-Latency Local Embeddings**.
